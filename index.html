About us

Mi Panaderia is a modern hispanic-style pastry/coffee shop established in the heart of phoenix to bring the taste of mexican culture to arizona. We offer our clients a full scope of sweet breads, coffee, traditional drinks and baked goods. Pop in for a visit and enjoy famous conchas of mexico!

Mexican Conchas

Conchas are ubiquitous in Mexican culture. They are available at virtually every Mexican bakery (panadería), where they are usually the most sold sweet bread. Enjoyed by people of all ages, conchas can be consumed at any time of day, whether it be for breakfast or for a midnight snack.
